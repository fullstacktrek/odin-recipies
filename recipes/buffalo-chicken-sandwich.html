Buffalo Chicken Sandwich

The Sandwich

This is a crockpot Buffalo chicken that is great for a hearty sandwich that will please those who love Buffalo chicken wings. It is ideal for watching football and can be topped with blue cheese or ranch dressing.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast Buffalo halves
  • 1 (17.5 fluid ounces) bottle Buffalo wing sauce, divided
  • 1/2 (1 ounce) package dry ranch salad dressing mix
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 6 hoagie rolls, split lengthwise

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place chicken breast in the slow cooker; pour in 3/4 of the wing sauce and ranch dressing.
  3. Cover and cook on low for 6 to 7 hours.
  4. Shred chicken in the cooker with two forks. Stir in butter.
  5. Pile shredded chicken and sauce onto hoagie rolls. Serve with remaining Buffalo sauce.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
